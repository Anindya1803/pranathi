Key Takeaways

Make It Obvious

The 1st Law of Behavior Change. Fill your environment with cues for your desired habits.

Make It Attractive

The 2nd Law. Pair an action you want to do with an action you need to do.

Make It Easy

The 3rd Law. Reduce friction. Decrease the number of steps between you and your good habits.

Make It Satisfying

The 4th Law. Give yourself an immediate reward when you complete your habit.

Identity Change

True behavior change is identity change. Decide the type of person you want to be.

Systems > Goals

Goals are about the results you want to achieve. Systems are about the processes that lead to those results.
